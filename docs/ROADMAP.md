# MAPORIA — Project Roadmap

This document lists ideas and potential features for development after the core MVP is stable.

## Social & Community
- Add friends & follow system
- Feed of friends' notable discoveries
- Collaborative challenges (team exploration)

## Rich Content
- Expand historical catalog with curated articles
- Partner with local guides & tour operators
- In-app mini-guides for POIs
- Challenges: Weekly or seasonal exploration events (e.g., “Discover 10 temples”).

## Advanced Mapping
- Offline map tiles and offline POI catalogs
- Heatmaps of popular areas
- Route recording & export GPX
- Trip Recording Mode: Optional route recording and export.

## Monetization & Rewards
- Cosmetic store (themes, avatar items)
- Premium features (detailed analytics, historical content packs)
- Sponsored curated trails (partner content)
- XP Store: Exchange earned XP for cosmetic items.

## Cross-platform
- Web dashboard for map portfolios
- Embedded widget for blogs & travel pages
